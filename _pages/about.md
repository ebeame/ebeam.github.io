# Yibin Wang

Yibin Wang (Student Member, IEEE) received the B.Eng. degree in Materials Processing from Harbin Institute of Technology, Weihai, China, in 2018, the M.Sc. degree in Materials Science from Carnegie Mellon University, Pittsburgh, PA, USA, in 2019, and the Ph.D. degree in Materials Science and Engineering from the School of Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, China.
